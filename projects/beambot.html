This is my BeamBot project, it's a downloadable client to make your custom bot, all you have to do is edit the Start.bat or Start.sh.
the program arguments are 'username' is your username, 'password' is your password, '+DEBUG' is to enable debug mode,
'ch=channel' to join a channel different channel than the given username ('channel' is the channel you want your bot in).
More help will be added later!
