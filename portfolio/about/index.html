About

This website is made in Jekyll using a customised version of Minima

Why not wordpress or wix?

I prefer Jekyll over things such as Wordpress or Wix. While they are easier to make simple websites or blogs with I do not want to be associated with such tools.

Jekyll converts markdown to html and applies it to a template. This is perfectly acceptable to do for a front-end based blog or website. Wordpress on the other end has everything in PHP and and thus will load slower and break easily.

Why Jekyll?

Jekyll is easy to setup, you can override themes and create your own layouts. In other words it is fully customisable.

You write posts, collections and pages completely in markdown and jekyll will convert it to html and put it in a layout template. You can make the layout yourself or use a theme to do it for you. This makes it much easier than creating a CMS or write an program to convert such files yourself.

I would recommend Jekyll for anyone who wants to create a blog or a simple website.
