Who am I

I am a student from the Netherlands. I started programming as a hobby, making silly things like mods and plugins in a game called Minecraft. Those mods were written in Java, so it took me a pretty long time to understand what was happening and how to create new things by myself.

After a couple of years I went to study Application Development which would focus on web development. I was taught HTML, CSS and JavaScript as well as PHP. I learned how to work with frameworks such as Laravel and developed an interest in Node.js. With the rising popularity of progressive web apps I am currently learning and using Angular in my web based projects.

After graduating I went on to study Informatica (Informatics) at Hogeschool Rotterdam. I choose for this education because I want to expand my knowledge on the subject matter I learned while studying Application Development. When I finish this study I will probably be searching for work, I currently already have a part-time job so I wont lack a lot of experience.
